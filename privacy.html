Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 2026

Bold Men of God (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Bold Men of God mobile application, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide you with the Bold Men of God experience:

  • Your first name — entered voluntarily when you first open the app, used only to personalize your experience (e.g., “Good morning, Jason”)
  • Bookmarks and reading progress — devotionals you have bookmarked and days you have completed, stored so your progress is saved across sessions
  • Push notification preferences — whether you have enabled daily reminders and your preferred time, if applicable

We do not require you to create an account with an email address or password. We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or any other device data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Personalize your in-app experience with your name
  • Save your bookmarks and reading progress so they persist between sessions
  • Send daily devotional reminders if you have enabled notifications
  • Provide and maintain the app’s core functionality

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your name, bookmarks, and reading progress are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Supabase stores data on secure servers and complies with industry-standard security practices. You can learn more at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

Bold Men of God uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database for storing your bookmarks and reading progress
  • RevenueCat (for paid subscribers) — processes and manages subscription payments on Android. RevenueCat does not receive your name or reading data. You can review their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Bold Men of God is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your name and reading data at any time
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings
  • Ask what data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your name, bookmarks, and reading progress for as long as you use the app. If you request deletion of your data, we will remove it from our systems within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
